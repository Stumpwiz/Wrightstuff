Rudolph Pugliese

Son of Joe and Jamie.

Rudolph Anthony Pugliese was born 8/22/16 in Austin TX. At nine months, Rudy and his family relocated back to MD. Concerned that his son would not be born on Maryland soil, Rudy's father had his father-in-law collect some Maryland soil and send it to him. While the nurses were not looking, Rudy's father placed the container of Maryland soil underneath the bed where his mother was laboring. So while born in Austin, Rudy was born over MD soil. After the birth, as a nurse was cleaning up, she discovered to her horror this foreign container underneath the bed in what was supposed to be a sterile room. When we explained, she was only slightly mollified. It should be noted that Rudy's father got the idea for this from President LBJ who famously had TX soil placed underneath the bed when his daughters were born in MD.
