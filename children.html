Jesse Renny-byfield

Daughter of Anne Mayer. Married to Simon.

Jesse was born 05/15/1974 in Washington DC to Anne and Doug Deneen. Her family moved to San Diego before her first birthday. Jesse attended the University of California, Riverside where she earned her Ph.D. in Anthropology with her first husband, Kyle Karnes, in 2009. After a brief stint in the US Army, Jesse served three tours in Afghanistan embedded as a civilian contractor advisor for British troops in Lashkar Gah. After her divorce from Kyle, Jesse completed a course as a Master Brewer at UC Davis where she met Simon Renny-Byfield who was completing a postdoctoral program in genetics. They were married on 02/26/2016. After living in California for most of her life, from the coast to the inland deserts and mountains and from San Diego to Berkeley, Jesse moved with Simon to IA, where they lived and worked for about five years. They moved to Colorado Springs CO in 2021.

Joe Pugliese

Son of Katie and Greg. Married to Jamie. Father of Kathleen, Lucia, and Rudy.

Joe was born 4/20/1984 in Holy Cross Hospital in Silver Spring MD. Joe was raised in Greenbelt MD and graduated from Eleanor Roosevelt High School in Greenbelt. Joe attended University of North Carolina in Chapel Hill for three years to study Drama. At the start of his senior year he decided instead to go out into the world, traveling to Chicago IL where he found work as a laborer on construction sites. In the evenings, Joe began playing music at Open Mike nights around town and writing his own songs. This eventually led him to his career as a touring singer-songwriter in the Americana tradition. He has toured with Steve Earle, Josh Ritter, Levon Helm, Brandon Flowers of The Killers, James McMurtry, and Joe Ely among others. Joe also has a podcast, The Working Songwriter, where he interviews songwriters about their "inspiration, process, and general ups and downs on making a life in music". He has interviewed Chris Smither, Lucinda Williams, Ray Wylie Hubbard, Josh Ritter, Citizen Cope, Carl Broemel of My Morning Jacket, Ian MacKaye of Fugazi, Joan Osborne, Lee Ann Womack, Brandon Flowers, and Steve Earle among others. Joe resides in Cheverly MD with wife Jamie and their children Rudy, Lucia and Kate.
